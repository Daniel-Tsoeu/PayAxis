Getting Started

1
Enter the username and password given to you by your administrator, then click Login.
2
The system will automatically detect your role and take you to the correct screen.

If you are an Employee

3
When you arrive at work, press the green Clock In button. Your start time will be recorded.
4
When you leave, press the red Clock Out button. Your total hours for the session will be calculated automatically.
5
You can view your recent attendance history on your portal screen.

If you are an Admin

6
Use the Dashboard to see an overview of all employees and attendance.
7
Go to Employees to add new staff, view their details and hourly rates.
8
Use Attendance to monitor who is clocked in and manually clock out employees if needed.
9
Use Payroll to generate payslips for any employee over a selected period.
Contact your administrator if you have forgotten your credentials.
